A Warm Welcome !

"When I was a kid, my dad used to play his guitar and sing the song “Our House.” Back then, we didn’t have a place to truly call our own, and I often dreamed of having a space that felt like home. That dream grew into a hope—to create a place not just for me, but for others to feel welcome and at peace. That’s why I named it “Ourhouse.” Inspired by the lyrics from that song, “Our house is your home for as long as you need peace,” this home is my way of sharing calm, comfort, and belonging with everyone who stays here."

Ming Ming
