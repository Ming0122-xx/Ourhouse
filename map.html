From Francisco Bangoy International Airport

Francisco Bangoy International Airport (DVO), commonly known as Davao International Airport, is located in Barangay Sasa, Buhangin District, Davao City, Philippines. The airport is approximately 6.8 kilometers (4.2 miles) from the city center, making it a short drive of about 45 to 60 minutes, depending on traffic conditions. For accurate directions and real-time traffic updates, you can use Google Maps or Waze. These platforms will provide the most efficient routes based on current traffic conditions.
